Health Care

Script for Progressive/History of Agreement on Issue

I'm ---- -----, a constituent calling to thank Senator/Rep ___ for supporting affordable health care and ask for continued support. I'm one of 20 million Americans who count on a marketplace plan for insurance. If the Affordable Care Act is overturned, I will lose access to health care. I want the Senator/Representative to block attempts to repeal the ACA.

Script for Conservative/History of Opposition

I'm ---- -----, a constituent calling to ask Senator/Rep to support affordable health care. I'm one of 20 million Americans who count on a marketplace plan for insurance. If the Affordable Care Act is overturned, I will lose access to health care. I want the Senator/Representative to vote against repealing the ACA.
